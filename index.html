Aalim Muhammed Salegh College of Engineering

Aalim Muhammed Salegh College of Engineering (AMSCE) is a premier institution offering undergraduate engineering. The campus has five academic blocks specializing in CSE/IT, ECE, EEE, Mech/Civil, and a block for first-year students. The well-planned layout of the campus maximizes the utilization of space, providing a perfect atmosphere for learning and research.



Founded with the vision to nurture young engineers who will contribute to global technological advancements, AMSCE prides itself on its dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and excellent student outcomes.
